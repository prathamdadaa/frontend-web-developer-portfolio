about

I'm Pratham Dada, a frontend developer from Dhanbad, Jharkhand. After completing my 12th in Commerce, I chose computer applications over a conventional commerce path because I'd rather build things than just account for them.

I'm currently pursuing a BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) through IGNOU, India's open university — which means I'm learning on a self-driven schedule, often a few chapters ahead of the syllabus because I'm also teaching myself in parallel through real projects.

Right now my strength is the frontend: structuring pages with HTML, styling them properly with CSS, and making them interactive with JavaScript. I'm not stopping there — full-stack development is the destination, and every project below is a step toward owning a product from database to browser.
